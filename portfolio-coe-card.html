CoE & Governance

Power BI Centre of
Excellence

Client: Global Medical Devices Company

Designed a Power BI Centre of Excellence end-to-end — governance framework, tiered best practice guidelines for viewers, Pro developers, and BI team developers, training programme, and workspace architecture. Delivered the governance foundation: a SharePoint-based CoE hub with role-specific best practice guides, and contributed workspace standards covering naming conventions, DEV/Test/Prod environments, and premium capacity efficiency rules as part of the wider BI team.

User Governance Tiers
Viewer Consume published reports. No edit access. Sensitivity label awareness.
Pro Developer Build in DEV workspaces. Follow naming & model standards. Peer review required.
BI Team Developer Own PROD deployments. Manage capacity, RLS, certified datasets & CoE standards.
Workspace Pipeline
DEV
Active development & iteration. Pro license only. No shared capacity.
Test
UAT & performance review. Peer sign-off required before promotion.
Production
Premium capacity. BI team governed. Certified datasets & RLS enforced.
